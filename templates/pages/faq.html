What is a Night Plan Kenya Profile, and how can I create one?

A Night Plan Kenya Profile is a personal account on Night Plan Kenya. When you join Night Plan, you get a Profile. This is where you can connect with friends and businesses, post updates on your city’s Nightline, or create group chats to plan your night out with friends.
To create a profile, download the Night Plan Kenya app and signup with Facebook, Twitter, or your email address. Once you sign up, you will be taken through the steps to create and activate your Night Plan Kenya Profile.

I don't see any offers for tonight on my app, Why?

Don’t worry, Night Plan Kenya can still be your guide to the latest trending events in your city. First, make sure all of your Filters on the Tonight view are turned off. Second, try logging out and logging back into the app. It helps to kill the app before logging back in, as this assures you have a fresh app state. Still no specials? Please message us on Twitter or email nightplankenya@gmail.com with your issue and we will look into it right away.
